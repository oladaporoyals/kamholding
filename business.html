Business

Nigeria

KAM HOLDING is Headquartered in Nigeria since 1997 and has manufacturing setup to cater for diverse products primarily for Construction Industry Products such as - Wire Nails, Roofing Nails, Twisted Nails, Couper Nails, Binding Wire, BRC, A142, Roofing Sheets, Stone Coated Roofing Tiles, Steel Wire Rod in Coils, TMT Bars, Sections - H-Bean, U-Channel, Granite & Stone Dust.

India

KAM opened its office in India, Asia in 2014 to further expand the International Trading activities and spread its business across the Globe.

China

The Group expanded its manufacturing arms to China to capture Asian market and started a manufacturing unit in year 2008 to produce Nail Caps. We also opened an Import/Export unit to cater for procurement of Machineries and Spare parts.
