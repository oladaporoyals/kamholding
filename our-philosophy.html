OUR PHILOSOPHY



OUR VISION




To be a Global Leader in Steel and Building Materials manufacturing with a Brand name that is synonymous with excellence.


OUR MISSION



To provide high quality building material products of international standard through Ultra-modern technologies, well trained professionals & highly motivated personnel.


OUR CORE VALUE


Self-Improvement



As an organization we seek to keep improving on our performance because we realize that this is the only way we can achieve our objective of having the major market share across all our steel product line. We train and encourage all aspiring employees till they attain their maximum potential. All members of staff at KAM Group are encouraged to strive for self-improvement by obtaining relevant professional qualifications to boost workplace knowledge and competency.

Professionalism



As a manufacturing company we shall at all times uphold the ethics of the profession while ensuring that we engage in the best practices. All employees are also expected to exhibit the highest ethics of their profession in relationships and interaction both within and outside the company.

Employees welfare



At KAM Group we recognize that the employees are very important and we always ensure that the welfare of all employees are adequately taken care of.
